# Manage Websites

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/01-manage-websites.webp" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

View and manage all your websites in your Elements Hosting account, including your primary site, addon domains, subdomains, and domain aliases.

Click on the website you'd like to manage, or select `Manage` from the `...` menu (we'll use elementsweb.site as an example for the following pages), and you'll be directed to its dashboard within your panel.
