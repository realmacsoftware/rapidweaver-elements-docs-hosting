# Elements Hosting

#### Welcome to Elements Hosting! 🎉

Elements Hosting was created for one simple reason: to give RapidWeaver Elements users a better, easier, and more reliable place to host their websites.

Unlike traditional hosting providers, we don’t just rent you server space and send you on your way. We build the website builder and we run the hosting platform. That means tighter integration, smarter defaults, and a setup that’s designed specifically for the way Elements websites work.

No complicated control panels. No slow servers. No “why doesn’t this work on my host?” moments.\
Just publish your site and move on with your day.

***

#### Built together. Optimized together.

Because we control the entire stack from the app you design in to the servers your site runs on, our hosting is tuned specifically for Elements-built websites. That means faster load times, better performance, and fewer surprises.

Behind the scenes, Elements Hosting runs on modern, high-performance infrastructure with NVMe SSD storage, generous system resources, and carefully chosen optimizations. For most users, this simply means their site feels fast and responsive. For the more technically curious, yes, it’s properly optimized, not shared-hosting-as-usual. 😉

***

#### Support that actually understands your website.

When something goes wrong, the last thing you want is to be bounced between your app developer and your hosting provider.

With Elements Hosting, there’s no disconnect. The same team that builds RapidWeaver Elements also understands how your site is hosted, deployed, and served to the world. That means quicker answers, better troubleshooting, and help that actually makes sense in the context of your project.

***

#### Hosting without the hassle.

Elements Hosting is designed for designers, freelancers, small businesses, hobbyists, and anyone who just wants their website online, without becoming a server administrator.

Security, updates, performance, and reliability are all handled for you. You focus on building your site. We’ll handle the rest.

And because Elements Hosting is built alongside RapidWeaver Elements, this is just the beginning. Deeper integrations and smarter features become possible when everything works as one platform.

***

#### Made for the Elements community

Elements Hosting isn’t generic web hosting. It’s hosting made for you, for the way you build, publish, and maintain websites with RapidWeaver Elements.

Simple when you want it to be. Powerful when you need it.

Welcome to Elements Hosting! 💙 ❤️ 💜
