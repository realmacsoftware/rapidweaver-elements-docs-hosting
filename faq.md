# FAQ

Welcome to the official FAQ for Elements Hosting. This resource is designed to provide you with clear and concise answers to the most common questions.

Whether you’re just getting started or you’re an experienced web hoster, our goal is to make sure you have all the information you need to manage your web hosting successfully and with confidence.

### Getting Started & Setup

<details>

<summary>How do I sign up for a hosting account?</summary>

Visit our [website](https://elementshosting.io/) to view our different hosting packages. Select the hosting package the fits your needs and then proceed with the checkout process.

Once your account has been provisioned, you will receive a welcome email containing your account details and next steps.

If you encounter any troubles or have any questions before, during, or after the signup process don't hesitate to contact us, we're always happy to help!

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I move an existing Elements site to Elements Hosting?</summary>

Yes! The easiest way is to update your publishing settings in the Elements app to your new Elements Hosting details, and then re-publish your website to your new hosting account with us.

We also offer free website migrations from your old web host to us if needed, just ask and we'll be happy to help!

</details>

<details>

<summary>How do I publish my site from RapidWeaver Elements to my new Elements Hosting account?</summary>

Follow our step-by-step [tutorial here](elements-hosting-reactor-panel/websites/manage-websites/ftp-accounts/). It covers publishing your RapidWeaver Elements site directly to Elements Hosting.

</details>

<details>

<summary>How long does it take for my site to go live?</summary>

Once your DNS has been pointed to Elements Hosting and has propagated, your site should immediately go live. If you need to update DNS, propagation typically completes within a few minutes to a couple of hours.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Is there a trial or money-back guarantee?</summary>

There’s no free trial, but we offer a 30‑day money‑back guarantee on all our hosting packages.

If you purchase/register a domain name with us, those are non-refundable due to the nature of how domain name registrations work. Please choose your domain name carefully, as they cannot be changed or refunded after purchase. Generally domain names are registered annually, and you can select whether to renew them or not.

</details>

### Security & Backups

<details>

<summary>Is my website secure?</summary>

Yes. All Elements Hosting sites include free SSL certificates powered by Let’s Encrypt, so your site is served securely over HTTPS by default.

In addition, we use security software and protective measures designed to guard against common threats such as malware, viruses, DDoS attacks, and abusive bot traffic.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Do you protect against malware and attacks?</summary>

Yes. Elements Hosting includes multiple layers of protection to help safeguard your website.

This includes malware and virus protection, DDoS mitigation, and bot protection, along with ongoing monitoring to help reduce the risk of attacks and keep your site running smoothly.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Do you provide backups?</summary>

Yes. Elements Hosting provides multiple layers of backups to protect your websites and project files, each serving a specific purpose:

* **Server-level backups** are full snapshots of the entire server, stored by our cloud storage provider. These backups are intended for disaster recovery and cannot be used to restore individual files or accounts. They allow recovery of the entire server in the event of a major issue.
* **Full account backups** are stored in Cloudflare R2 object storage and capture all websites and data within your hosting account. You can restore these backups yourself via the Elements Hosting Reactor Panel, or request a restore from the Elements Hosting team. Individual files cannot be restored from these backups, restoration is always for the entire account.
* **Project file backups** save your RapidWeaver project file each time you publish your site from the Elements app. The backup is stored on your hosting account and overwritten with the most current version after each publish. This ensures you always have an up-to-date copy of your project file available for restoration if needed.

Together, these three backup layers provide comprehensive protection for both your website content and your project files.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I restore my site from a backup?</summary>

Yes. You’ll be able to restore your site from a full account backup and a project file backup.

This gives you flexibility to recover from mistakes, or restore your site if something unexpected happens.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What happens if something goes wrong?</summary>

If something goes wrong, you’re not on your own.

Our team is here to help diagnose the issue, guide you through recovery options, and assist with restoring your site from backups when needed. Because we manage both the hosting platform and understand how Elements sites work, support is faster and more straightforward.

</details>

### Technical Questions

<details>

Our hosting supports HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and server-side databases. Apache, PHP, and MariaDB power our platform. If your custom code or scripts fit within that environment they can usually run fine. That said, we can’t officially troubleshoot or maintain custom code.
